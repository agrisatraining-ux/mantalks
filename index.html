About the Initiative

Many men carry unspoken emotions.
We built a place to let them breathe.

Stress, loneliness, doubts, or quiet struggles — they rarely find a safe outlet. ManTalks creates a confidential, judgment-free space where men can connect anonymously, in English and Tamil , to share what's on their minds.

This is not therapy. This is not counselling.
This is a listening circle — a place to be heard.

🔒

Anonymous

Pseudonyms welcome. Camera optional. Your identity is yours to keep.

🌏

Bilingual

Sessions available in English and Tamil.

👥

Peer-Led

Real humans, real conversations. No professionals, no prescriptions — just connection.

💬

Structured

Facilitated 45–60 minute slots, progressing from 1-on-1 to small groups over time.

"

Our vision is to normalize emotional openness among men, foster supportive communities, and create a brotherhood of listening across languages and cultures.

SESSION GUIDELINES

The only rules here are
respect and honesty.

✓

Respect confidentiality

What is shared here stays here. Always.

✓

No judgment, trolling, or abuse

This is a safe space. Abusive behaviour will result in removal.

✓

Peer support — not therapy

This is a listening circle. Not a substitute for professional help.

✓

Respectful presence is mandatory

Camera use is optional, but showing up with full attention is not.

✓

Advice only when asked

Sometimes people just want to be heard. Don't fix — just listen.

✓

You may leave at any time

No pressure. No guilt. Your comfort comes first — always.
